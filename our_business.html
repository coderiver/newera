New Era is a leading renewable heat energy producer in Ukraine. All of our produced heat is supplied to kindergartens, schools, hospitals, municipal buildings and commercial enterprises in Ukraine. The Group’s current total installed heat capacity is 2.1 MW.

New Era is always in search for new opportunities in order to develop new energy efficient projects.

Heat Production statistics:
