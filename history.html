The New Era Energy Group

The New Era Energy Group (New Era) is a leading renewable heat energy and biomass producer in Ukraine. Currently operating 2.1 MW biomass heat stations and 26,000 tons annual pellet production, New Era invests, constructs and manages renewable heat stations and pellet production facilities and sells eco-friendly heat to municipal schools, hospitals, kindergartens and industrial enterprises.

New Era gives priority to medium and large-sized projects that improve Ukraine’s energy efficiency and independence.

New Era provides a full-range of services:

  • Production and transportation of heat energy Production and transportation of heat energy

  • Wood pellet production

  • Cultivation of energy willow

  • Manufacture of heat facilities

  • Installation of boiler equipment

  • Maintenance of heat facilities

  • GSM automatization of heat facilities

Being the both operator and manufacturer of heat facilities, New Era provides superior and lower cost services to its clients.
