The New Era Energy Group

We are a leading renewable heat energy and biomass producer in Ukraine. New Era focuses on green projects that reduce natural gas consumption and provide positive environmental effects. The Group currently owns and operates 2.1 MW biomass heat stations generating over 1,500 Gcal of clean heat energy annually.
