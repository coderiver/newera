Board of Directors

Andrey Supranonok

Chairman of the Board

Andrey Supranonok is an accomplished investment banking professional with strong leadership skills and experience. He currently heads Jaspen Capital Partners, a distinguished Ukrainian investment banking firm. Mr. Supranonok began his career at Lehman Brothers in London before joining BNP Paribas in London as a principal trader. He left Great Britain to return to his native homeland of Ukraine, where for several years he was the Deputy CEO and Head of Sales and Trading at Phoenix Capital in Kiev.

While in Ukraine, Mr. Supranonok has played a principal role in several of the country’s largest capital market transactions,. Andrey holds a Master’s Degree in Economics from University College London.

Jon McKee Queen

Non-Executive Director

Jon McKee Queen has 15 years of financial sector experience, during which time he has served on the Board of Directors of several companies. He currently serves as a Managing Director with Jaspen Capital Partners, a distinguished Ukrainian investment banking firm. Mr. Queen began his career as a licensed securities representative for John Hancock Financial Services in New York. After graduating law school, he worked for several years as an attorney at Latham & Watkins LLP and participated in numerous large public and private sector financial transactions worth over $2 billion.

Mr. Queen departed Latham to work in the Hong Kong commodities and trading sector, first as Senior Vice President to the International Environmental Trading Group, then as Executive Director to The Anemone Group. He served as an official advisor to the National Agency of Ecological Investments of Ukraine from 2008 until 2010. Directly prior to joining Jaspen Capital Partners, Jon worked at Phoenix Capital Investment Bank (Ukraine) as a Director and Head of International Sales. Mr. Queen also worked on Capitol Hill for the United States House Banking and Financial Services Committee during President Clinton’s administration.

Jon holds an Economics degree from Cornell University and a Juris Doctorate degree in Law from the University of Pennsylvania, as well as a Certificate of Study in Business & Public Policy from Wharton Business School.
